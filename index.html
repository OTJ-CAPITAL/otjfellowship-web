The opportunity

The hedge fund industry is built on talent. Mathematicians, engineers, and researchers who understand markets deeply enough to build systems that beat them consistently.

That talent exists in Africa — in every university, in every city. It just needs infrastructure, mentorship, real problems to solve, and a visible path into the industry.

OTJ Fellowship creates that path. Fellows work on real systems. Real strategies. Real capital. Not simulations.

OTJ Fellowship is that path.

Program

Three tracks. One goal.

All tracks work on the live OTJ Capital infrastructure. This is not classroom learning — it is real work on real systems.

12 Weeks

Quant Research

  • Alpha strategy design and hypothesis testing
  • Backtesting and walk-forward validation
  • Statistical modelling and signal research
  • Live strategy deployment on OTJ Capital
  • Factor analysis and attribution modelling

12 Weeks

Engineering

  • Trading system architecture and design
  • Real-time market data pipelines
  • Risk engine development and testing
  • API design and production deployment
  • Performance optimization at scale

8 Weeks

Fund Operations

  • Portfolio construction and rebalancing
  • Risk management and reporting
  • Investor communications and dashboards
  • Regulatory compliance fundamentals
  • Capital allocation and redemption flows
